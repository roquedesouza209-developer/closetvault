Security Notes

What this MVP protects today.

  • Passwords are hashed with scrypt before they are written to disk.
  • Uploaded file contents are encrypted at rest and only unlocked during an active session.
  • Secure link sharing and deeper synchronization controls are intentionally staged as next steps.
